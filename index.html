About us

D&J Landscaping and General Handyman Services specializes in a wide range of handyman services in Stratford-upon-Avon and neighborhood. From basic repairs and maintenance to landscaping and gardening, we have skills and experience to keep your property in top shape. Our team is dedicated to providing high-quality services and ensuring customer satisfaction.

  • Do you lead a busy lifestyle and don't have enough time to do all your homework?

  • Do you love your garden, but are unable to maintain it?

  • Do you need someone to do a handyman job that requires skills and tools you don't have?

We work with you to make your dream come true, ensuring the project runs smoothly from the beginning of the design process to the finished garden. Each project is unique, starting from luxurious gardens, where plants grow and ending with the maintenance of large public places. Prices are reasonable and affordable, individual approach to each client.
